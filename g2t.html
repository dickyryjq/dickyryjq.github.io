Product design for GoToTraining

GoToTraining provides a virtual classroom for live online training and education. Since I joined the team, I've been working as the design lead for important new features including Cloud-Based Recording, In-session activities, onboarding flows, etc. In the fast-paced agile development environment, every day I collaborate with awesome folks in Engineering and Product Management teams to define and prioritize product requirements. I also work with editors to come up with in-product languages, work with UX researchers to conduct usability tests.

I also designed for iOS app and desktop endpoint.
