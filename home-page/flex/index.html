Redesigning a vending machine

This is a final project in Prototype Lab course, 2013.

Usually, vending machines have very simple interface and little feedback. Users have to select and pay multiple times for different kinds of snacks, and sometimes stand in front of a vending machine realizing they don’t have cash. In this project, shopping process is more detailed, and brings two ways of paying: cash and bankcard. The project went through lo-fi to hi-fi with paper, Balsamiq, and Flex prototype. During the iterations, each stage was tested with users.
