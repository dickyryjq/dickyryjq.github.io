IMPAQD

**This is an ongoing project.

Timeline: Mar 2014 – May 2014

My major contributions: Ideation, website redesign, wireframing.

This spring semester, I took Designing and Leading a Business course in Tepper School of Business. My client, an MBA student, is working on a startup – IMPAQD, which is a real-time GPS enabled app that creates a platform for carriers to interact directly with shippers, thereby eliminating the need for intermediary brokers in the freight industry. This ability creates a new market for shippers while developing new sources of revenue for the carriers.

Survey
We designed a survey to gauge the shippers’ need and preference. Questions includes “Usually how long in advance do you schedule shipping with truckers/brokers?” “How strong do you think your relationship to the brokers is?”

Ideation
Standing at a customer’s perspective, we brainstormed ideas to get people know about our product, use it, and keep using it. The funnel has all the questions we need to think about, and we wrote brainstorming ideas on post-its and put them together.
