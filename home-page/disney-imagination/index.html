Disney Imagination

The actual drawing and rendering of this project was completed in 12 hours.

We are team Allegheny Pirates, a team of four entering the Disney Imagination 2014 competition. Our challenge was: Select a large and densely populated urban area and design an experience that temporarily or permanently transforms the city for the enjoyment of its citizens and visitors. Although we didn’t make it to the final, this is a fun project we gained lots of experience and is very valuable.
We designed a ride: “The Search for the Golden Parrot” at Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania

My roles:

Team Lead, Illustrator & visualizer.

My contributions:

Drawing & Graphic, Coloring, and Story telling.

Pittsburgh once stood tall as a bastion of industrial excellence but the city gradually evolved into a vibrant technological hub that draws from a diverse pool of talent. The large university population as well an energized startup community render Pittsburgh a city constantly in flux. In the spirit of this dramatic change, we propose a reimagined glance at some of Pittsburgh’s most iconic locations, enclosed within a building that honors Pittsburgh’s industrial heritage: the steel mill.

The ride we have devised allows guests to visit the reimagined locations within the steel mill through a playful narrative involving one of Disney’s most memorable characters: Iago. The lovable parrot’s charismatic appeal and outspoken nature make him an ideal interactive guide. The ride follows Iago, as he explores Pittsburgh in search of an elusive “Golden Parrot”, with helpers (the guests) assisting him along the way.

Story behind the design:

Iago, the lovable parrot, is lounging around the Egyptian desert when a falling scroll catches his eye. On it are an assortment of indecipherable glyphs along with the words “Golden”, “Parrot” and “Pittsburgh”. Iago quickly connects the dots and endeavors to scour Pittsburgh in search of the Golden Parrot. Iago begins his exploration of Pittsburgh with two helpers (the guests) he recruits to assist him along the way. Iago’s search for the Golden Parrot proves fruitful early on as he spots the treasure tucked away in the recesses of the Robotics Institute. Upon discovering the Golden Parrot Iago realizes the golden treasure may have a mind of its own. 

Guest Experience:

The guest experience takes place within a reimagined version of one of Pittsburgh’s iconic steel mills. Enclosed within the building are iconic Pittsburgh landmarks that are either completely redesigned, enhanced by technology, or made accessible through a novel perspective. Guests become familiar with Iago’s pursuit of the Golden Parrot through a set of murals they are exposed to as they walk to the loading zone of the ride. The events depicted in the mural are then retold in a short video clip that plays on the dashboard screen of each vehicle. Guests follow Iago in their vehicle as he ‘flies’ from one reimagined landmark to another. Throughout the ride, Iago also calls upon the guests to perform various tasks to assist him in his pursuits.
