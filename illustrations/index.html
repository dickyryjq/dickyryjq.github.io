Illustrations

Yes, I really began to draw when I was a 4-year-old girl. The passion drives me to learn traditional Chinese painting and calligraphy, and later, oil painting, sketching, color pencil drawing, crayon drawing, etc. Sadly, when I was young, digital cameras are not accessible to everyone in China and a majority of my paintings were lost without an electronic form.
 
In the gallery below, images are in chronological order. You will find comic strips when I was in low grade in primary school, the earliest work obtainable. =)
