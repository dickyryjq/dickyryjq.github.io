Why Travel Getaway?

After a Competitive Analysis of iPad e-magazines, I found that the travel magazine market is mostly focused on high-budget trip and middle-aged audience.
In my design, I choose the target audience who are usually not catered: older readers, students, and families.
 

Travel Getaway is an iPad Magazine for family friendly and low-budget travel.

Budget travel for older readers:
- Inexpensive
- Calm, quiet, and peaceful places
- Less crowded
- Natural environment
- More convenient

Budget travel for students:
- Refreshing/exotic
- Cheaper
- Exclusive travel destinations not usually
found in other magazines
- Mysterious and fun

Family-friendly travel:
- Locations that all family members can participate in and enjoy
- Needs to work within a budget
