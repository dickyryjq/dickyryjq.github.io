Travel Getaway

An iPad Magazine for family friendly and low-budget travel.

After a competitive analysis, I found that the travel e-mag market is mostly focused on high-budget trip and middle-aged audience. Thereby, in my design, the target audiences are older readers, students, and families. In the design phase, I iterated more than 5 rounds. I also kept a complete set of process materials, which includes competitive analysis, personas, moodboard, design language, sketches, text experiments, grid options, intermediate designs, etc.
 
Enjoy the final design!
