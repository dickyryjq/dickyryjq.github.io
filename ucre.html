Designing Tools for TA Office Hour

Queue Manager, a well-scheduled office hour experience.

We designed tools for TA session in School of Computer Science, CMU. This is a project for User-Centered Design and Evaluation course. We did this project while learning contextual design, which includes Observation and Interpretation, Affinity, Model consolidation, and Visioning. I hand drew storyboards for the team after synthesizing the data and coming up with visioning.
