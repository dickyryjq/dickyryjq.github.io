My 20 years illustrations

I began to draw when I was a 4-year-old girl. The passion drives me to learn traditional Chinese painting and calligraphy, and later, oil painting, sketching, color pencil drawing, crayon drawing, etc. I also won a lot of awards throughout my childhood. Sadly, when I was young, digital cameras are not accessible to everyone and a majority of my paintings were lost without an electronic form.
The first six pictures you see are my drawings during college.
